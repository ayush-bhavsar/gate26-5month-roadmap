This 16-week phase is dedicated to building a strong conceptual understanding of each subject from the ground up. Focus on learning the theory, solving practice problems, and making concise notes.

Over these 3 weeks, your goal is to thoroughly revise all subjects and test your knowledge with subject-wise tests. Analyze your performance to identify and strengthen your weak areas.

The final 3 weeks are for simulating the actual exam environment. Focus on time management, accuracy, and analyzing your performance in full-length mock tests to build confidence for exam day.

General Tips for Success

  • Consistency is Key: Stick to the schedule as much as possible. A few hours of focused study daily is more effective than cramming.
  • Create Short Notes: Make concise notes for quick revision, focusing on formulas and key concepts.
  • Solve PYQs Religiously: Use Previous Year Questions to understand the exam pattern and question types.
  • Analyze Your Mistakes: After every test, spend time understanding your errors. Categorize them to identify weak points.
  • Stay Healthy: Ensure you get adequate sleep, eat nutritious food, and get some physical activity to keep your mind sharp.
